About me

Originally from Wales, I have been living in Liverpool for the past 10 years. In that time I have travelled around the world from Europe to Fiji.
After coming back from travelling I started a new career as a freelance portrait and wedding photographer. Which I have grown my confidence, my creative side and ability to adapt to changing environments. During lockdown this ability to adapt to changing environment has been very crucial during the recent pandemic.

I started to learn more about marketing, IT and different coding languages during lockdown as, I'm not a person who likes to sit around I like to keep busy and love to learn.
My most recent achievements have been learning to build a website and build games from scratch, through Code Nation bootcamp.
I have learnt lots of different code languages Javascript, CSS, HTLM, Python, React and I'm still learning more.

I am very proud of everything I have learnt in such a short time and a thirst to learn more. Technology is such a powerful way to connect with millions of people and ever growing and I want to be part of that world.
My passion comes from creating something from nothing to visually or mentally stimulating I can see this through my photography work, Websites building and game creation.
